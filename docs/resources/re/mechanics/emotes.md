# :material-emoticon-excited: Special Emotes

## Try Your Luck

The "Try Your Luck" emote from the [Grab Bag](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2944860/Granblue_Fantasy_Relink__Emote_Expansion_Set_Grab_Bag/) DLC Pack mimics rolling from the base game.

3 different emotes are available (SSR, SR and R Crystals - with [obj id 0xF0319](../obj_id.md)).

The chances are hardcoded:

* SSR = 3%
* SR = 15%
* R = 82%
