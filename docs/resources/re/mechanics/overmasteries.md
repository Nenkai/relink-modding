# :material-star: Overmasteries (Limit Bonus Meditation) Rates

Overmasteries let you gain 4 extra stats that are randomly generated.

## Available Bonuses per Tier

It is not possible to roll more than one of a specific stat, unless it exists multiple times in the pool.

Data is extracted from `limit_bonus_mediation_category` table.

### Tier 1

* Attack Power Up
* Health Up
* Critical Hit Rate Up
* Stun Power Up
* Skill Damage Up
* Skybound Art Damage Up
* Chain Burst Damage Up
* Normal Attack Damage Cap Up
* Skill Damage Cap Up
* Skybound Art Damage Cap Up
* Healing Cap Up
* Attack Power Up (Duplicate 1)
* Attack Power Up (Duplicate 2)
* Attack Power Up (Duplicate 3)
* Attack Power Up (Duplicate 4)
* Health Up (Duplicate 1)
* Health Up (Duplicate 2)
* Health Up (Duplicate 3)
* Health Up (Duplicate 4)

### Tier 2/3

* Attack Power Up
* Health Up
* Critical Hit Rate Up
* Stun Power Up
* Skill Damage Up
* Skybound Art Damage Up
* Chain Burst Damage Up
* Normal Attack Damage Cap Up
* Skill Damage Cap Up
* Skybound Art Damage Cap Up
* Healing Cap Up
